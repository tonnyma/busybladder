THE GOAL STUDY

Do you ever have a sudden and urgent need to pee (urinate)?

Do you sometimes leak urine by accident
and you can’t
control it?

If so, you may have urinary incontinence (UI) due to an overactive bladder

We’re looking for 108 patients for the GOAL STUDY

Will you be 1 of

We are hoping 108 children and teenagers with the same condition as you will take part, in different countries around the world

The GOAL study will test a medicine that is used in adults with the same condition. It is now being tested to find out if it is safe for children and teenagers to use, and if it will help their UI

If you join the GOAL study, your participation can last up to approximately 2 years. You may withdraw from this study at any time.
